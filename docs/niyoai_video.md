# Niyo AI 1‑Minute Product Video — ULTRA-CONVERSION STORYBOARD

## Overall Goals
- **Pattern interrupt** in first 2 seconds that stops the scroll.
- Create emotional investment through visceral before/after contrast.
- Rapid-fire visual storytelling with cuts every 1-2 seconds.
- Weave social proof and urgency throughout, not just at the end.
- Challenge the viewer to take action, don't just ask politely.

## Advanced Tone & Delivery Notes

### Voice Direction
- **NOT corporate. NOT salesy. NOT robotic.**
- Think: **Brutally honest mentor** who's giving you the truth you need to hear
- Delivery: Fast but CLEAR. Punchy. Confident. Occasional dramatic pause for effect
- Energy: High but controlled—like a TED talk, not an infomercial
- Use REAL numbers and specifics—never round or hedge ("about 40 hours" → "forty hours")

### Pacing Strategy
- **Cuts every 1-2 seconds maximum**
- Use speed ramps: slow down on KEY NUMBERS, speed up on transitions
- Beat of silence after shocking stats (let them land)
- Accelerate into CTA—create momentum toward action

### Music Architecture
- **0-7s**: Tense, anxious (minor key, clock ticking, building pressure)
- **7-11s**: Tension release, hope enters (key change to major)
- **11-40s**: Upbeat, confident, modern (think tech product launch energy)
- **40-47s**: Brief tension return (question mark moment)
- **47-54s**: Urgency builds (faster tempo)
- **54-60s**: Resolution, empowerment (definitive ending, not fade)

### On-Screen Text Hierarchy
**CRITICAL (must be visible):**
- $127K, 47 seconds, 94% accurate, 3.8x faster, "Stop reading resumes"

**SECONDARY (quick flashes):**
- Specific timelines, individual stats, "No credit card"

**SUBTLE (almost subliminal):**
- Feature labels, UI elements, support text

### Visual Effects Arsenal
- **Speed ramps**: Slow-mo on impressive moments, then snap back
- **Split screens**: Show contrast (old way vs Niyo)
- **Parallax**: Create depth, make UI feel alive
- **Glitch effects**: Use sparingly on "pattern interrupt" moments
- **Heat maps**: Show where Niyo finds key skills in CVs
- **Counter animations**: Numbers rolling up create hypnotic effect
- **Zoom bursts**: Emphasize shocking stats

### Color Psychology
- **Red/chaos tones**: Problem state (0-7s, 47-54s)
- **White space/clarity**: Solution state (7-11s, 54-60s)
- **Blue/green (trust colors)**: Product demos (11-47s)
- **Gold/highlight**: Social proof, winning moments (33-40s)

## Conversion Optimization Tactics Embedded

1. **Loss Aversion**: Open with cost of bad hire, close with opportunity cost
2. **Social Proof**: Real testimonials at emotional peak (33-40s)
3. **Scarcity**: Implied competition ("while you're screening, they're hiring")
4. **Specificity**: 47 seconds, $127K, 94%—never round numbers
5. **Risk Reversal**: No credit card, instant test
6. **Identity**: Position viewer as decisive leader, not slow follower
7. **Pattern Interrupts**: Black screen open, thought bubble smash, multiple pace changes
8. **Micro-commitments**: Each section gets a mental "yes" before moving forward
9. **Challenge close**: Dare them to try, don't beg
10. **Visual metaphors**: Empty seat, competitor ahead—make abstract concrete
