Why Current Pricing Underperforms

"Credit Anxiety"

Recruiters don't think in PDFs or credits. They think in jobs, shortlists, and time saved. Uncertainty kills conversion.

Punishing Spikes

Hiring is bursty. Variable costs for spikes cause anxiety instead of rewarding usage.

Feature Parity

When only volume changes, buyers anchor to the cheapest plan. No clear incentive to upgrade early.

Selling Infrastructure

"Pay for file processing" vs "Pay for faster shortlisting". You're selling the mechanism, not the outcome.
