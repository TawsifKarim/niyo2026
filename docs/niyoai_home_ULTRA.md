## COPYWRITING VOICE & TONE GUIDE

### What This Sounds Like:

**NOT:**
- Corporate/salesy: "Leverage AI-powered solutions..."
- Hypey: "Revolutionary! Game-changing!"
- Robotic: "Our platform utilizes advanced algorithms..."

**YES:**
- **Brutally honest mentor**: "You're not slow. The system is broken."
- **Confident without arrogance**: "ChatGPT helps you think. Niyo helps you hire."
- **Specific, not vague**: "47 seconds" not "lightning fast"
- **Conversational but sharp**: "Look, you're skeptical. Good."

---

### Delivery Rules:

1. **Short sentences.** Punch. Impact.
2. **Use "you" not "users" or "customers"**
3. **Active voice only** ("Niyo finds them" not "They are found by Niyo")
4. **Real numbers** (94%, $127K, 47 sec) not round estimates
5. **Contrast pairs** (old way → new way, chaos → clarity)
6. **Questions that resonate** ("Still screening CVs manually?")
7. **Challenge, don't beg** ("What are you waiting for?")

---

## VISUAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES

### Color Psychology:

- **Red/chaos tones**: Problem sections (2, 9)
- **White space/clarity**: Solution sections (3, 8, 10)
- **Blue/green (trust)**: How it works, social proof (4, 6)
- **Gold/highlight**: Stats, success metrics (6)

---

### Typography Hierarchy:

**HERO:**
- H1: 72px, bold, high contrast
- Stats: 48px, medium weight, color accent

**BODY SECTIONS:**
- H2: 36px, bold
- Body: 18px, line-height 1.6 (readability)
- Callouts: 24px, color blocks

**STATS:**
- Numbers: 64px, bold
- Labels: 14px, uppercase, letter-spacing

---

### Motion & Animation:

- **Counter animations**: Numbers rolling (hypnotic, proves scale)
- **Speed ramps**: Slow-mo on impressive moments, snap back to fast
- **Parallax scrolling**: Depth, modern feel
- **Fade-in-up**: Sections appear as you scroll (not all at once)
- **Hover states**: Buttons pulse subtly (call to action)

---

### Mobile-First Considerations:

- Hero must work in 3 seconds on mobile (even faster attention span)
- Stats stack vertically on mobile
- Comparison tables become accordion on small screens
- CTAs sticky on mobile (always visible)
- Video demos auto-play muted on mobile scroll
